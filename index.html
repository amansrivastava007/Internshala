How to play Internshala Fantasy cricket!!!

Welcome

Step 1: Create a new team by navigating through the menu bar or simply pressing CTRl+N

Step 2: Give name to your team and press OK.Selction box will appear.

Step 3:Select the players from the category of Batsmen,Bowler,Allrounder and Wicketkeeper.Remember,there is selection procedure i.e Not more than 5 Batsaman or Bowlers can be selected.Maximum of 3 Allrouders can be selected.Only one Wicketkeeper can be selected.Also one very important thing to remember is all players have their credit score.A total of 1000 credit is given to make the team you cannot excede the credit score.

Step 4:After you are done making your team save it through menu bar or a shortcut of CTRL+s will work.

Step 5: You can review your save team by opening it through the menu bar or CTRL+O

Step 6:Now that you have made your team it's time to see how your players performed.Click on evaluate option from menu bar or CTRL+E.Evaluation window will appear.Now select your team and match no.Press claculate and it will display the point given by individual player and also the complete team.

Step 7:You can quit the application anytime by clicking Quit option in menu bar.

Well now you know the game go and play!!!
